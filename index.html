Our story

Not just a bar.
A local.

Hippies was born out of a simple frustration: why is it so hard to find an honest, affordable, and unpretentious bar with great food in Singapore? We wanted a place where the bartender remembers your pour and strangers leave as regulars.

We're a community of drinkers, not just a counter to drink at. Pull up a stool, work your way through a changing spirit list, catch the music, and make a friend or two.
