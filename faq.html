Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before you pull up a stool.

Do you really charge no GST and no service charge?
Yes. What you see on the menu is exactly what you pay. Our $19.90 nett Ribeye steak is exactly $19.90 when the bill comes. We believe in honest pricing without the nasty surprises at the end of the night.
Do I need to make a reservation?
Walk-ins are always welcome, but tables do go fast on weekends and steak nights. If you’re coming with a group or want to secure an outdoor table, we highly recommend booking online or dropping us a WhatsApp at 8881 8391.
Are there vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free options?
Yes, we do! While we are known for our grills and steaks, we offer several delicious vegetarian and dietary-friendly options. Just let our friendly staff know about your dietary requirements when you arrive, and we’ll take care of you.
Is there parking available near the bar?
There is street parking available along Guillemard Road and the surrounding residential streets. However, spots can fill up quickly during peak hours, so taking public transport or a Grab is always a good idea (especially if you plan on enjoying our Happy Hour!).
When is Happy Hour?
Our Happy Hour runs every day before 8:00 PM. It’s the perfect time to grab a Bucket of 5 San Miguel Pilsners for just $40 nett.
