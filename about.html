Our Story

More than just
a counter to drink at

We built Hippies because we wanted a place we'd actually want to hang out in. No pretentious vibes, no hidden fees. Just cold beer, hot grills, and a solid community.

Born from frustration

Hippies was born out of a simple, shared frustration: why is it so incredibly hard to find an honest, affordable, and unpretentious bar with genuinely great food in Singapore?

We were tired of paying a premium for a pint, only to be slapped with GST and service charges on top of an already bloated bill. We wanted a local spot—a true neighbourhood joint right in the heart of Guillemard Road where everyone feels welcome.

The Local Philosophy

We operate on a straightforward philosophy: what you see is what you pay. Our $19.90 nett Ribeye steak isn't a gimmick; it's a staple that keeps people coming back. We pour perfect pints of Guinness and mix serious cocktails without the serious attitude.

At Hippies, the bartender remembers your pour. We play classic rock, our outdoor seats are perfect for unwinding, and strangers frequently leave as regulars. It's not just a bar; it's a community.
